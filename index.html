About us

The Biomedical Data Analysis and Visualisation (BDAV) Lab focuses on developing machine learning, statistical, and computational techniques to analyze and interpret multimodal biomedical data. Our aim is to enable precision medicine and biomedical discovery by integrating information across imaging, omics, clinical, and electronic health record data.

Prof Jinman's research is in the intersection of machine learning for biomedical image analysis and visualization (part of AI), with a focus on multi-modal biomedical data processing. CIX Kim continuously publishes in top venues in his field and received multiple competitive grants and scientific recognitions. Prof. Jinman is actively involved in his research communities where he is an Associate Editor of CMPB and Visual Computer, and a reviewer for major journals and conferences in his field.
